EMOTIONS

Emotions are feelings that we experience in response to situations, people, or thoughts.

1.1 LOVE

Love is a strong feeling of care, affection, and connection toward someone or something.

1.2 HATE

Hate is a very strong feeling of dislike, anger, or rejection toward someone or something.

1.3 ANGER

Anger is a strong emotion you feel when something upsets, hurts, or frustrates you.

1.4 EXCITEMENT

Excitement is a strong feeling of happiness, energy, and anticipation about something good or interesting.
